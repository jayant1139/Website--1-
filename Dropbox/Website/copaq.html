COPaQ 2022

Conference on Optics, Photonics & Quantum Optics

An International Symposium on Optics (XLV OSI SYMPOSIUM) will be held at at IIT Roorkee during 10-13 November, 2022 to foster interactions among the broad disciplines in Optics. The conference will be themed on Optics, Photonics and Quantum optics with short courses to be held on 9th November,2022.

Topics

Variety of topics such as non-linear optics and nano and bio photonics will be discussed along with tutorials for better comperhension of students.

Keynote Speakers

We will be inviting some important researchers across the globe and from our country.

Program

The event will last from 10 to 13 Nov 2022. Checkout Program for exact schedule.
